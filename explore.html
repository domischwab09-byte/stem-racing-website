Engineering Journey

This page maps our complete workflow from early concept sketches to race execution. Every phase has objectives, measurements and clear ownership.

Design Philosophy

We optimize for low drag, stable launch behavior and manufacturing repeatability. Instead of only chasing a single fast run, we focus on reliable performance.
